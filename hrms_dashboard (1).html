Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for leave?

Go to Leave Management from the sidebar, fill out the leave application form with your leave type, dates, and reason. Your manager will be notified automatically.

How do I download my payslip?

Navigate to My Payslips from the sidebar. Click on the download icon next to any payslip to save it as a PDF.

How do I request attendance correction?

Go to Attendance page and click "Request Correction" button. Fill in the details and submit. HR will review and update.

How do I update my profile?

Click on My Profile in the sidebar. You can view your details. To edit, click the "Edit Profile" button (requires manager approval for certain fields).
